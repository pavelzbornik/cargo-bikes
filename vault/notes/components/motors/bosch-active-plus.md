## Bosch Active Line Plus

The Bosch Active Line Plus (often referred to commercially as the Active Plus) is an entry-level mid-drive motor from [[Bosch]]'s eBike Systems portfolio. It sits above the base Active Line in the lineup, adding a cadence sensor for a more natural pedalling feel, while remaining below the Performance Line and [[Bosch]] Cargo Line units in terms of torque output and intended load capacity. It is well suited to compact urban cargo bikes and lighter-duty cargo applications.

## Overview

The Active Line Plus uses a cadence sensor rather than the torque sensor found on higher-end Bosch units. This means motor assistance is keyed primarily to pedal rotation speed rather than applied force, producing a more consistent but less dynamically responsive power delivery. For urban commuting and light cargo use, this is generally acceptable and keeps system cost lower.

It is compatible with Bosch's range of displays and control units (Purion, Intuvia, Kiox and their Smart System equivalents, depending on generation), allowing riders to select assist modes (Eco, Tour, Sport, Turbo on most configurations).

- **vs. Bosch Performance Line CX ([[Bosch]])** — The CX delivers 85 Nm via torque sensing and suits heavy cargo bikes; the Active Line Plus trades power for lighter weight and lower cost.

## Notes

- The Active Line Plus is considered an entry-point motor for e-cargo applications; heavier cargo bikes (longtails, box bikes) typically require the Cargo Line or Performance Line CX.
- Bosch has progressively migrated its product line toward the Smart System platform; confirm generation compatibility when speccing replacement parts or displays.
