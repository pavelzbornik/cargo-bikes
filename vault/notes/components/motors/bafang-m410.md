# Bafang M410

## Overview

The Bafang M410 is a mid-drive crank motor from [Bafang](/components/motors/bafang.md)'s M-series lineup, positioned as an accessible, cargo-capable drive unit suitable for longtail and utility e-bikes. It sits below the flagship [[Bafang M620]] (Ultra) in Bafang's hierarchy, offering a lighter and more compact package while still delivering meaningful torque for loaded riding.

## Market Position

The M410 occupies Bafang's mid-range cargo segment — more refined than entry-level hub motors, but more cost-accessible than the [[Bafang M620]]. Its 80 Nm torque output is sufficient for moderate cargo loads and urban hilly terrain, making it a practical choice for longtail bikes targeting everyday utility riders rather than heavy-duty cargo haulers.
