# Shimano E7000

Part of the [[Shimano]] STEPS mid-drive system family, the E7000 is a 250 W / 60 Nm motor targeting urban, trekking, and light cargo applications. It sits above the entry-level E5000 and below the higher-torque cargo-specific EP6/EP8 units.

- **Trail** - mid-level assist
- **Boost** - maximum power

## Position in the Shimano STEPS Lineup

The E7000 is a mid-tier urban/trekking motor. It delivers smooth, quiet power suitable for load-carrying city use but does not offer the 85 Nm torque of the cargo-focused **EP6** or the lighter high-performance **EP8**. Newer cargo bike designs increasingly specify EP6/EP8; the E7000 remains relevant on bikes where quiet, smooth urban assist is prioritized over raw torque.
