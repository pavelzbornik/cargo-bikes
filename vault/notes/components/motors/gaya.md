## Gaya

Gaya is a French electric cargo bike manufacturer specializing in affordable rear-hub motor cargo bikes designed for families and urban commuters. The brand focuses on making cargo cycling accessible with competitive pricing and practical designs.

## Motor System

Gaya uses proprietary **rear-wheel hub motors** rated at 250W, compliant with EU e-bike regulations. Their hub motor approach keeps costs lower than mid-drive alternatives while providing reliable pedal assistance for urban riding and moderate loads.
