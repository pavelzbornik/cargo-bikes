# Child Seat (1-2 seats)

The Onesport Child Seat is a brand-specific accessory designed exclusively for the [[Onesport]] [[OneSport OT01]] cargo bike. It enables the rear cargo platform to accommodate one or two child seats, transforming the bike into a capable family transportation solution for daily use.

## Description

Purpose-built for family transportation, this child seat configuration allows parents to carry one or two children securely on the rear platform of the OneSport OT01. The accessory is engineered to integrate directly with Onesport's proprietary mounting system, ensuring a stable and reliable fit without the need for adapters or aftermarket hardware.

## Features

- Supports 1 or 2 child seats on the rear cargo platform
- Designed specifically for the Onesport OT01 frame and mounting interface
- Provides a secure, stable base engineered to Onesport's load specifications
- Flexible configuration to suit single or dual child transport needs
- Designed to complement the OT01's family-oriented cargo carrying capacity

## Compatibility

This accessory is brand-specific and designed exclusively for use with Onesport bikes. It is compatible with the following model:

- [[OneSport OT01]]

As a brand-specific product, it is not intended for use with bikes from other manufacturers. Always confirm compatibility with your specific OT01 variant and verify that the total payload remains within the bike's rated maximum load capacity when seating two children.

## Pricing

Pricing ranges from approximately €100 to €300 depending on configuration (single or dual seat) and regional availability. Contact an authorised Onesport dealer or visit the official Onesport website for current pricing and order details.

## Notes

- The number of seats that can be fitted (1 or 2) may depend on the platform size and the bike's maximum payload rating — always check total load limits before configuring for two seats
- Child seats may need to be purchased separately and confirmed compatible with the Onesport platform mounting system
- As a brand-specific accessory, third-party universal child seats (e.g., Yepp, Bobike, Thule) may not be directly interchangeable without additional adapters
