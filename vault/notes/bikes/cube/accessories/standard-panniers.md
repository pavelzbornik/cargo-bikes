## Standard Panniers

## Overview

The Standard Panniers are a brand-specific accessory designed by [[Cube]] for use with their Longtail Hybrid family of cargo bikes. The extended rear carrier is engineered to accept two standard pannier bags per side, significantly increasing the load-carrying capacity of the bike for everyday transport, shopping, and family use.

## Description

The Cube Longtail Hybrid platform features an extended rear carrier that is purpose-built to accommodate standard pannier bags in a double-stacking configuration — two bags per side. This design maximises the usable cargo volume available on the rear of the bike without requiring custom or proprietary bag systems, allowing riders to use widely available standard panniers alongside the existing rack infrastructure.

## Features

- Extended rear carrier accepts two standard pannier bags per side
- Double-pannier configuration per side for high cargo volume
- Compatible with standard pannier mounting systems
- Designed specifically for the Cube Longtail Hybrid platform
- Integrates natively with the bike's rear rack geometry

## Compatibility
