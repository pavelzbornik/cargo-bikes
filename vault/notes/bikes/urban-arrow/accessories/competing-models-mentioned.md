# Urban Arrow Pet Carrier

A brand-specific pet transport accessory designed by Urban Arrow for use with their longtail cargo bikes, including the [[Urban Arrow Breeze]]. Built to integrate directly with the Breeze's rear cargo platform, it provides a secure and ventilated space for transporting cats, small dogs, and other small animals.

## Description

The Urban Arrow Pet Carrier is engineered specifically for Urban Arrow's longtail lineup. Unlike universal third-party carriers, this accessory is purpose-built to mount cleanly onto the Urban Arrow Breeze's rear rack system, ensuring a stable, wobble-free ride for the animal and a tidy fit on the bike. It is not cross-compatible with other cargo bike brands.
