## E-Mog E-City+

![E-Mog E-City+](https://i0.wp.com/www.emog-bikes.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/02/velo-cargo-suspension-e-city.jpg?fit=1124%2C1124&ssl=1)

## Overview

The E-Mog E-City+ is the premium, refined iteration of the compact longtail cargo e-bike family. Building on the proven E-City platform, the E-City+ introduces a front suspension fork that significantly improves comfort and handling while maintaining the sub-2-meter wheelbase essential for urban agility. Designed for families requiring reliable daily transport with enhanced ride quality, the E-City+ offers European engineering, practical cargo capacity for dual children plus supplies, and accessibility for a wide range of rider heights (1.50m–1.90m).

### Dimensions & Weight

- **Frame material:** Steel
- **Frame size:** One size (accommodates 1.50m–1.90m riders)
- **Wheelbase:** Less than 2 meters (highly maneuverable)

## E-City+ vs. Standard E-City

The primary upgrade from the standard E-City to E-City+ is the **front suspension fork**, which:

- Absorbs vibrations and impacts from urban terrain
- Improves handling and control on uneven surfaces
- Increases rider comfort on longer journeys
- Adds approximately €50–100 to retail cost
- Slightly increases maintenance compared to rigid frame version

All other specifications (motor, battery, brakes, cargo capacity) remain identical.
