## Peugeot e-Longtail

![Peugeot e-Longtail](https://lesveloselectriques.fr/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/avis-e-longtail-peugeot-1140x694.webp)

## Overview

The Peugeot e-Longtail is a family-focused electric longtail cargo bike developed through a collaboration between Peugeot Cycles (over a century of cycling expertise) and Douze Cycles (cargo bike specialists). This e-longtail is engineered to carry up to 2 children or 1 adult passenger, making it an ideal choice for urban families seeking a versatile, well-equipped transportation solution. The bike combines a powerful Shimano EP6 Cargo motor with an innovative automatic transmission system, offering an impressive value proposition at its current promotional price of €3,599 (down from the original €5,100 MSRP).

## Technical Specifications

### Dimensions & Weight

- **Frame material:** Aluminum (frame manufactured in Portugal)
- **Frame size:** One size
- **Total weight:** 32.5 kg (with battery installed)
- **Load capacity:** 200 kg total system capacity
  - Rear rack: 80 kg max
  - Front rack: 18 kg max (optional front basket)

### Motor & Power

- **Motor:** Shimano EP6 Cargo (mid-drive/crank motor)
- **Power output:** 250W continuous
- **Maximum torque:** 85 Nm
- **Sensor type:** Torque sensor (provides natural pedal assistance modulation)
- **Boost/Walk Assist:** Yes (Walk Assist mode for controlled pushing up slopes or in tight spaces)

### Battery & Range

- **Capacity:** Shimano 630 Wh (integrated, vertical mounting on frame)
- **Configuration:** Single battery (non-removable integrated design)
- **Charging time:** 6 hours for full charge
- **Estimated range:** 50–90 km depending on assist level and conditions
  - **ECO mode:** Maximum range, moderate assistance for battery conservation
  - **TRAIL mode:** Balanced performance and range
  - **BOOST mode:** Full power for loaded starts and steep climbs

### Drivetrain & Gears

- **Type:** Chain drive
- **Gears:** 10-speed Shimano Cues Di2 automatic transmission
  - **Automatic mode:** System automatically selects appropriate gear based on pedal force and terrain
  - **Manual mode:** Can override automatic to select specific gear manually
- **Transmission design:** Electronic, automatically adjusts gear ratios for optimal performance

### Brakes

- **Type:** Hydraulic disc brakes (Tektro Gemini HD-M535)
- **Front rotor:** 180 mm
- **Rear rotor:** 180 mm
- **Performance:** Good stopping power and modulation; reviewers noted effective braking even when fully loaded with 2 children

### Wheels & Tires

- **Wheel size:** 24-inch (front and rear)
- **Tire model:** Schwalbe Pick-Up (premium "balloon" profile)
- **Tire benefits:** Wide profile provides excellent shock absorption and vibration damping on rough surfaces

### Suspension

- **Front suspension:** None (rigid fork)
- **Rear suspension:** None (rigid)
- **Trade-off:** Lightweight design and simplicity, but less comfort on severely roughened or potholed roads

### Lighting & Safety

- **Front light:** Busch & Müller LED (integrated, battery-powered)
- **Rear light:** Integrated LED with brake-light function (brightens when braking)
- **Frame lock:** Included (cafe-style frame lock for quick, secure parking)
- **GPS tracker:** Not included
- **App lock:** Not available

## E-bike Features

### Assist System

- **Modes:** 3 levels of electric assistance (ECO, TRAIL, BOOST)
- **Sensor:** Torque sensor for responsive, natural pedal feel
- **Walk Assist:** Available for controlled pushing (extremely useful on slopes or when maneuvering in tight spaces)
- **Max speed:** 25 km/h (EU standard for e-bikes)

### Display & Connectivity

- **Display:** Shimano e-tube display (simple, clear, real-time performance data)
- **Information shown:** Current speed, battery level, assist level, trip distance, calories burned
- **App integration:** Optional E-tube Project app (Shimano) allows detailed trip tracking and performance monitoring
- **Note:** App does not currently support custom assist level programming (unlike Bosch systems)

### Safety & Weather Resistance

- **Weather resistance:** Sealed electrical connectors; suitable for urban use in various weather conditions
- **Lighting:** Integrated front and rear lights powered by main battery
- **Stability features:** Reinforced kickstand; rear seats and side tubes provide secure passenger seating

## Real-world Performance

### Range & Power

- **Tested range:** Approximately 70 km average in mixed urban/suburban riding with 2 passengers (~40 kg combined)
- **Motor responsiveness:** Shimano EP6 Cargo is highly responsive with minimal lag; provides smooth acceleration even from standstill when fully loaded
- **Assist progression:** Natural feel with torque sensing; assistance increases with rider effort and terrain demands
- **Hill performance:** Excellent; 85 Nm torque easily handles steep urban grades with passenger load
- **Throttle/boost:** Walk Assist mode extremely valuable for pushing bike up steep driveways or repositioning in tight parking spaces

### Comfort & Handling

- **Rider comfort:** Comfortable riding position; adjustable seat (Selle Royal saddle)
- **Handling:** Responsive and predictable; weight distribution is well-balanced despite significant rear mass
- **Rigidity trade-off:** Lack of suspension creates noticeability on heavily potholed or root-strewn paths, but acceptable for typical urban riding
- **Noise levels:** Quiet operation; Shimano motor and automatic transmission are nearly silent
- **Rear passenger comfort:** Padded rear seats designed for 2 children; side tubes and footrests keep passengers secure and comfortable on long rides

### Cargo Stability

- **Rear rack stability:** Very stable even with 2 active children bouncing or shifting weight
- **Learning curve:** New riders must adapt to subtle weight shifts from rear passengers (especially young children); this becomes intuitive after a few rides
- **Turning radius:** Standard for longtails; requires anticipation on sharp urban turns with passenger load
- **Maneuverability:** Good; compact enough for city streets despite cargo-carrying capability

## Cargo Capacity & Use Cases

### Load Capacity

- **Total system capacity:** 200 kg (includes rider, passengers, and cargo)
- **Rear rack:** 80 kg (suitable for 2 children or 1 adult passenger)
- **Front rack:** 18 kg (optional front basket)
- **Tested configuration:** 2 children (~40 kg combined) + adult rider; bike handled with ease

### Use Cases

1. **Urban family commuting:** School runs, errands, weekend family outings
2. **Multi-child transport:** Designed specifically for families with multiple children; pre-equipped rear seats and footrests
3. **Mixed cargo:** Groceries + 1–2 passengers simultaneously
4. **Hilly terrain:** Strong Shimano EP6 motor excels on urban slopes
5. **Short- to mid-range trips:** 50–90 km range suitable for daily urban commuting

### Passenger Configuration

- **2 children (typical):** Easily accommodated; aged 3–7 reported comfortable and secure on test
- **1 adult passenger:** Within weight limit; requires more stability anticipation from rider
- **Vertical storage capability:** Innovative design allows upright, space-saving storage when not in use (important for urban dwellers with limited space)

## User Reviews & Experiences

### Community Feedback

#### Pros

- **Excellent value for money:** Superior components compared to competing models at similar price points
- **Automatic transmission innovation:** Shimano Cues Di2 automatic gearing eliminates need for manual shifting; especially useful for inexperienced cyclists and when managing passengers
- **Powerful, responsive motor:** Shimano EP6 Cargo delivers smooth acceleration and handles heavy loads without strain
- **Pre-equipped for family use:** Includes rear seats, footrests, and side tubes; no expensive aftermarket seat purchases required
- **Good range:** 50–90 km sufficient for urban commuting and family trips
- **Lightweight for cargo segment:** 32.5 kg is notably lighter than competing cargo bikes (typically 38+ kg)
- **Vertical storage:** Innovative design saves space in apartments and tight storage areas
- **Accessibility for novices:** Walk Assist feature invaluable for new riders and slope management
- **Quiet operation:** Minimal noise from motor and transmission

#### Cons

- **No front suspension:** Rigid frame creates noticeable vibrations on heavily potholed or root-strewn paths (main complaint from reviewers)
- **Brake rotor size:** 180 mm rotors adequate but reviewers prefer 200 mm for fully-loaded descents
- **Non-removable battery:** Cannot swap battery for extended range; charging at home only
- **Lack of app customization:** Cannot adjust assist levels via E-tube app (unlike Bosch systems)
- **Learning curve for passengers:** Young children require time to learn proper positioning and balance on rear seats
- **Motor power entry-level:** EP6 Cargo is solid but less powerful than flagship Shimano EP8 Cargo (though torque output is identical at 85 Nm)
