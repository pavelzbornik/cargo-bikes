## Which Insurer for Your Profile?

### Budget-conscious, standard use

**Assurancesvelo or Hepster** (190-250 EUR/year). Higher deductible, depreciation applies, but significant savings.

### Active rider, wants zero surprises

**Laka** (230-270 EUR/year). Zero deductible, zero depreciation, battery included. Full replacement cost always.

### Family with child passengers

**MAIF + separate personal accident policy** (450-540 EUR/year total). Only major insurer with passenger injury coverage. Add a Garantie Accidents de la Vie for complete family protection.

### Frequent traveler

**Qover** (265-275 EUR/year). Europ Assistance partnership provides best-in-class roadside help across France and Europe.

## Deductible Impact

| Deductible    | Typical Premium  | Out-of-Pocket on 800 EUR Claim |
| ------------- | ---------------- | ------------------------------ |
| Zero          | 250-275 EUR/year | 0 EUR                          |
| 50 EUR fixed  | 210-240 EUR/year | 50 EUR                         |
| 100 EUR fixed | 190-220 EUR/year | 100 EUR                        |
| 10% of value  | 210-240 EUR/year | 300+ EUR                       |

The premium difference between zero and 50 EUR deductible is typically 30-40 EUR/year.

## Lock Requirements

All insurers require at least one certified U-lock attached to a fixed point. Some require two locks. Always verify specific requirements before purchasing a policy.
