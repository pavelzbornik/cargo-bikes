# Electric Cargo Bike Insurance

A practical guide to insuring your electric cargo bike, with a focus on the French market.

## Legal Context

Electric cargo bikes (250W, 25 km/h) are classified as regular bicycles in France and most EU countries. Insurance is not legally mandatory, but strongly advisable for bikes worth 3,000 EUR or more given theft rates and replacement costs.

## Coverage Types

### Theft Protection

- Covers full replacement cost up to policy limit
- Requires certified lock (U-lock) attached to a fixed point
- Coverage applies while locked outside or on vehicle racks

### Damage and Breakage

- Accidental impact, collision, environmental damage
- Deductible options: zero, fixed amount (50-100 EUR), or percentage of value
- Battery damage typically included

### Battery Coverage

- Battery theft and failure coverage increasingly important
- Replacement batteries cost 300-800 EUR
- Some insurers bundle at no extra cost; others charge 20-50 EUR/year

### Roadside Assistance

- Taxi transport if bike is unrideable (especially valuable for heavy cargo bikes)
- Repair coordination, locksmith services
- Quality varies significantly between providers

### Passenger Injury Protection

- Critical for families carrying children
- Rarely included in standard bike insurance
- May require separate personal accident policy (Garantie Accidents de la Vie in France, ~100-150 EUR/year)

## Choosing a Policy

### Key Evaluation Criteria

| Factor                | What to Check                                   |
| --------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| **Deductible**        | Zero vs. fixed vs. percentage of bike value     |
| **Depreciation**      | Zero depreciation vs. 2-5% annual reduction     |
| **Battery**           | Included or optional add-on?                    |
| **Assistance**        | Quality and response time of roadside help      |
| **Claims limit**      | Annual cap (typically 6,000 EUR, 2 claims/year) |
| **Lock requirements** | Number and type of locks required               |

### Recommended Approach for Families

1. **Specialized bike insurer** for theft, damage, and battery (200-300 EUR/year)
2. **Separate personal accident policy** for passenger injury coverage (100-150 EUR/year)
3. **Total: 350-450 EUR/year** for comprehensive family protection
