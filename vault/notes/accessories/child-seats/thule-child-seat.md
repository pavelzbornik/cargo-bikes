## Description

A universal rear-mounted child seat from Thule, a leading Swedish outdoor and transport brand. Suitable for children from approximately 9 months (once the child can sit unaided) up to around 6 years / 22 kg. Designed to mount on standard rear racks, making it compatible with a wide range of cargo and longtail bikes. Certified to EN 14344 European safety standard. Priced at €119.

## Features

- Five-point safety harness with padded shoulder straps
- Adjustable footrests with retention straps
- Integrated spoke guard for child foot safety
- Ergonomic padded seat shell
- Rear rack mounting system (compatible with standard rack tube diameters)
- Reflective details for low-light visibility
- EN 14344 certified
- Compatible with optional Thule raincover and windshield accessories (sold separately)
- Available in multiple colour options

## Compatibility

Universal third-party accessory made by Thule, compatible with standard rear racks across many cargo and longtail bike brands. Verify that your rack's tube diameter and maximum payload rating accommodate the seat. Confirmed compatible with the [[Longtail VG Life 672Wh]].

When mounting the seat, ensure the rear rack supports the combined weight of the seat plus child, up to 22 kg for the child alone in addition to the seat's own weight. Confirm that the rack's tube diameter matches the Thule mounting bracket specifications before installation.
