## MIK-HD Child Seat

A rear-mounted child seat using the MIK-HD (Mounting Is Key Heavy Duty) click-on interface. Designed to replace passive bolt-on seats, it offers tool-free installation and removal directly onto any rack fitted with a MIK-HD mounting plate, making it a practical active seating solution for younger children on cargo and longtail bikes.

MIK-HD is an open mounting standard promoted by the Thule/Yepp ecosystem and adopted by a range of European rack and accessory manufacturers. Any bike equipped with a MIK-HD compatible rear rack can accept this seat, regardless of brand.

## Features

- Tool-free click-on/click-off attachment to MIK-HD rack plate
- 5-point safety harness
- Padded interior with impact-absorbing shell
- Low rack-level seating position for improved bike stability
- Compatible with any MIK-HD rated rear rack
- Replaces passive (bolt-on) child seats — direct swap onto compatible racks

## Compatibility Notes

This is a **universal** accessory compatible with any bike fitted with a MIK-HD rear rack or mounting plate. It is not brand-locked to a single manufacturer.

### Rack Requirement

The bike must have a rear rack with an integrated **MIK-HD mounting plate**. Standard MIK racks (non-HD) may not be suitable for the heavier load ratings typical of longtail cargo bikes. If the bike ships with a passive child seat already fitted, that seat must be removed and a MIK-HD compatible rack installed before this seat can be used.

Rack manufacturers known to produce MIK-HD compatible racks include Racktime and Atran Velo, among others.

## Installation

1. Confirm the rear rack has a MIK-HD mounting plate.
2. Remove any existing passive seat.
3. Align the seat with the MIK-HD plate and press down until the click-lock engages.
4. Verify the seat is locked before placing a child in the seat.

To remove, press the release mechanism and lift the seat clear — no tools required.
