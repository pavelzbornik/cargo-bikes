## Baby Seat (Polisport)

Polisport is a Portuguese cycling accessories manufacturer with a well-established range of child bike seats covering both rear-mounted and front-mounted options. Their seats are designed as universal accessories compatible with a wide variety of bike brands, including cargo bikes, city bikes, and e-bikes. The range spans entry-level through premium models, with the €89–€149 bracket covering their mid-to-premium rear seats such as the Bilby and Koolah lines.

## Features

- **Safety standard:** EN 14344 certified (European child carrier standard)
- **Harness:** 5-point safety harness with padded shoulder and crotch straps (rear models)
- **Weight capacity:** Up to 22 kg (rear-mounted models)
- **Age range:** Approx. 9 months (child must support own head) to 7 years
- **Headrest:** Adjustable high-back headrest with side-impact protection
- **Footrests:** Adjustable footrests with anti-slip surface; closed toe-box on premium variants
- **Recline:** Multi-position recline adjustment available on mid and premium models (e.g., Bilby, Koolah)
- **Padding:** Removable and machine-washable seat cover
- **Reflectors:** Integrated rear reflectors for visibility
- **Mounting:** Standard ISO 11243-compliant rear rack mount; frame/seatpost mount available on RS variants (no rack required)
- **E-bike compatibility:** Explicitly rated for use on e-bikes; rack must support a combined load of at least 25 kg

## Compatibility

Polisport child seats are universal accessories designed to fit standard equipment across all bike brands:

- **Rear rack-mount versions** fit any ISO 11243-compliant rear rack, which is the standard found on most longtail cargo bikes, city bikes, and trekking bikes regardless of brand.
- **Frame/seatpost-mount versions** (Bilby RS) attach via a clamp to the seatpost or rear frame tube, extending compatibility to bikes without a rear rack.
- **Front-mounted models** (e.g., Bubbly Maxi Plus) fit standard 1-1/8" threadless steerer tubes or handlebar stems.

Compatible with [[Fatstreet Classic - Two-seater electric cargo bike (Female)]] when paired with an appropriate ISO-compliant rear rack rated for the combined weight of the seat and child.
